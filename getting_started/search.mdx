Ever had the desire to go back and look at what was discussed in a meeting? Or do you simply want to find certain meeting notes without having to look through all of your meetings manually?

No worries, Jamie has your back!

### How can I search for keywords?

In the top of your sidebar on the left, you enter any keyword and search across all your notes.
