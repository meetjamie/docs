### How to set up your output language in Jamie

The language of your meeting notes can be set and/or changed in your settings. Simply head to your summary settings and select the language that you prefer for your meeting summary. 

**GIF**

<Note>
**Important:** selecting your language in the settings will force the summary output to be in that language, meaning both action items and the summary, will be in that language. 

However, this does not change the language of your transcript, which will always be generated in the language of the meeting.
</Note>
