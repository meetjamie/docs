Connect Jamie to Slack to share meeting summaries, transcripts, and tasks directly to your team channels.

## Connecting Slack

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Slack**
2. Click **Connect Slack**
3. Select your Slack workspace and authorize the connection
4. Choose which channels Jamie can access

## Sending Meetings to Slack

![Integrations Card](/images/integrations-card.png)

1. Open a meeting in Jamie
2. Click the **Share** button and navigate to **Integrations**
3. Select **Send to Slack**
4. Choose a channel or direct message
5. Click **Send**

Your meeting notes are posted to the selected channel.

## Automatic Sync

Enable **Automatic sync** to send every meeting to a specific Slack channel automatically after processing completes. Toggle this in **Settings → Integrations → Slack**.

## Settings

Configure what content to include:

| Option         | Description                          |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **Summary**    | Meeting notes with executive summary |
| **Transcript** | Full meeting transcript              |
| **Tasks**      | Action items from the meeting        |

<Info>
  At least one option must be enabled.
</Info>
