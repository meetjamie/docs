In order for Jamie to be able to access the audio of your meetings, you would need to download and install the Jamie recorder.

You can do so by clicking on your workspace (top left) and then clicking on "Download app"

![](/DownloadRecorder.png)

Once you have downloaded the recorder, please double click on it and follow the installation process for your operating system.
